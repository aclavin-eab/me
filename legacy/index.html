I'm Andrew, welcome to my site.

I am a front-end developer with a background in graphic design. I see design as much more than brand identities, posters, and advertisements; it is a universal visual language. It helps me understand logical constructions of scripts, markup, and the interactive relation between the two.

Mixing new technology with art and design is what I enjoy most; that, sushi, modern art, and Woody Allen humor. Currently, I am searching for full time front-end development work in Chicago. Send me an email at andrewclavin8@gmail.com to talk anything web, art, sushi, or Woody Allen–related.
